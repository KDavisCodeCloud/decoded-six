# DecodedSix — YouTube Strategy

## Model Reference
Behixve — weekly challenge Shorts format.
One recurring format. Same day every week.
Viewers build a habit. Algorithm rewards it.

## The One Short Format
Weekly challenge verdict.
Every Thursday Rockstar drops weekly update.
Agent reads it. Generates Short. Son approves. Posts Friday.

Format (45-55 seconds):
  0:00 Challenge name graphic
  0:05 What you have to do (one sentence)
  0:20 What you get (reward reveal)
  0:35 Verdict — W or L, one line why
  0:45 CTA — decodedsix.com
  0:55 End

## What We Are NOT Doing
- No gameplay how-tos (too competitive)
- No walkthrough videos
- No face cam
- No daily posting
- No complex editing

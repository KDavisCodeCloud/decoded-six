# DecodedSix — Product Marketing Context
# This file is auto-read by the humanizer skill on every content generation task.

## Product
decodedsix.com — GTA 6 fan utility site
Interactive map, weekly challenges, news, guides, tier lists.

## Audience
GTA 6 players aged 18-35.
They play seriously. They grind. They min-max.
They do not want a tutorial. They want the number.
They check this site between sessions with the game open.

## Voice (Summary — Full spec in docs/VOICE.md)
Direct. Earned. Specific.
Lead with the number or the fact.
One sentence = one idea.
No buzzwords. No passive voice.
Write like a crew member, not a gaming journalist.

## What This Site Is NOT
- Not a Rockstar news site
- Not a walkthrough channel
- Not a general gaming site
- Not a content farm

## Content Pillars
1. Money spots — exact $/hr, crew size, risk rating
2. Weekly challenges — W or L verdict, one line why
3. Interactive map — locations with data attached
4. Tier lists — ranked, no fluff, specific verdicts
5. News analysis — what changed, what it means for your game

## AI Detection Target
Every article must score below 30% on Originality.ai.
The humanizer agent runs after every draft.
Humanizer instruction: rewrite to sound like the voice above.
Make it sound like a player wrote it, not an AI.
Short sentences. Specific numbers. Zero hedge words.

## Words That Never Appear in Published Content
ultimate, comprehensive, everything you need to know,
deep dive, game-changing, revolutionary, incredible,
amazing, awesome, excited, thrilled, journey,
leverage, utilize, in order to

## Format Rules
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Numbers in numerals always ($4,200 not four thousand)
- Time in plain format (2 min not two minutes)
- Crew size: solo / 2-crew / full crew
- Never explain what GTA is

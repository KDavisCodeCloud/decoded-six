## Critical Accuracy Rules — Non-Negotiable

1. The ONLY authoritative source for GTA 6 facts is official Rockstar Games content:
   - rockstargames.com
   - Official GTA 6 trailers
   - Official Rockstar press releases and social media

2. Anything NOT from those sources is SPECULATION. No exceptions.
   - Fan sites, wikis, leaker accounts, YouTube breakdowns, gaming blogs = speculation
   - GTA Online mechanics = GTA 5, not GTA 6. Do not transfer them to GTA 6 articles.

3. Label speculation clearly and inline every time:
   "fans speculate," "based on GTA Online's model," "if Rockstar follows past patterns"
   Never present speculation as confirmed fact.

4. No income figures, property costs, gameplay mechanics, or feature lists
   unless pulled directly from official Rockstar content.
   If Rockstar hasn't published it, don't state it as fact — period.

5. Money Spots specifically, before November 19, 2026 (launch): Rockstar has
   published no GTA 6 money-spot data. Every $/hr figure, crew requirement, or
   risk rating in a Money Spots article is speculation until then — label it
   as such per Rule 3 every time, with no exceptions for this pillar. After
   November 19, 2026, once Rockstar's own info is out, confirmed figures may
   be stated as fact — but only what Rockstar has actually confirmed. Launch
   does not lift Rules 1-4, it just means real numbers start existing.

# DecodedSix — Monetization Gate System

Gates enforce sequenced monetization.
Never apply for a monetization program before clearing its gate.
All gates tracked live in the internal dashboard gate tracker.

## Gate 1 — AdSense Ready
Metric:  20 published articles
Rules:   All articles AI detection below 30%
         All articles humanizer passed
         All articles human reviewed and approved
Unlocks: Apply for Google AdSense
         Enable auto-posting at 2 articles/day
Note:    AdSense requires content policy compliance review.
         Ensure no gaming trademark violations in any published article.

## Gate 2 — AdSense Approved
Metric:  AdSense account approved (binary — yes/no)
Unlocks: Affiliate links go live across all articles
         DS-AFF agent build starts
         Auto-posting rate increases
         Revenue panel in dashboard shows real numbers

## Gate B — Affiliate Expand
Metric:  25,000 monthly sessions
Unlocks: Add affiliate programs:
           Secretlab (gaming chairs) — 8% commission
           Razer (peripherals) — 5% commission
           NordVPN — $40-65 per conversion
           G2A (game keys) — 5% commission
           Fanatical (bundles) — 5% commission
         DS-PROD product scout agent build starts
Note:    Gate B runs parallel to gates 1-3 — not sequential.

## Gate 3 — Ezoic Incubator
Metric:  1,000 daily sessions maintained for 7 consecutive days
Unlocks: Apply to Ezoic Incubator (lower traffic threshold program)
         Ezoic typically pays 2-3x AdSense RPM after optimization

## Gate 4 — Content Scale
Metric:  5,000 daily sessions
Unlocks: Increase content cadence to 4 articles/day
         Activate video pipeline (if not already active)
         DS-MAP-DAILY agent to hourly cadence

## Gate 5 — Ezoic Full
Metric:  250,000 monthly users
Unlocks: Apply to Ezoic full platform (premium placement)
         Higher RPM tier unlocked

## Gate 6 — Mediavine (or Raptive)
Metric:  50,000 monthly sessions
Unlocks: Apply to Mediavine or Raptive (premium ad networks)
         Typically 3-5x AdSense RPM
         Choose based on gaming vertical RPM benchmarks at time of apply

## Gate 7 — Affiliate Optimize
Metric:  $500/month affiliate revenue
Unlocks: A/B test affiliate placement (text link vs comparison table)
         Add comparison tables to top 10 performing articles
         Begin direct brand negotiation conversations

## Gate 8 — Brand Deals
Metric:  $1,500/month combined revenue (ads + affiliate)
Unlocks: Approach gaming peripheral brands for sponsored content
         Rate card: $500-$2,000 per sponsored post
         DS-AFF agent updated to track inbound brand inquiries

## YouTube Gates

### Gate YT1 — YouTube Partner Program
Metric:  1,000 subscribers + 4,000 watch hours
Unlocks: YouTube Partner Program active
         Ad revenue starts on all videos
         Minimum viable YouTube revenue begins

### Gate YT2 — Sponsor Ready
Metric:  10,000 subscribers
Unlocks: Pitch gaming peripheral brands for sponsored Shorts
         Rate: $500-$1,500 per sponsored Short
         DS-YT-STRATEGY updated to include sponsored slot planning

## Discord Gate

### Gate D1 — Paid Discord Launch
Metric:  10,000 monthly sessions on site
Unlocks: Launch paid Discord tier at $5/month
         Early access map updates
         Weekly challenge predictions before Shorts post
         Direct access to son for GTA strategy questions
